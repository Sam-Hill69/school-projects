Mark Dean is best known for his pivotal role in the development of the IBM Personal Computer (PC). Alongside a team of engineers, he played a crucial part in creating the architecture for the IBM PC, which became the industry standard. Dean is also credited with co-inventing the first gigahertz chip and contributing to the development of the color PC monitor.
